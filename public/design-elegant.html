About the Doctor

DFW's Leading &
Most Trusted Neurologist

Dr. Nnamdi C. Dike, DO is a Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine with a specialization in neurology. Recognized throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex as the busiest and most sought-after neurologist in the region, Dr. Dike has built a reputation for extraordinary thoroughness, deep compassion, and a talent for identifying diagnoses that other physicians have missed.

Patients travel from across DFW and beyond to be seen by Dr. Dike, who takes the time to truly listen and explain complex neurological conditions in terms that empower patients to participate in their own care.
